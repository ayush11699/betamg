Welcome to Beta-Murungai

We, at Beta Murungai, aim to unleash the untapped nutritional and commercial value of Moringa as a crop to improve people's lifestyle. We simplify people's diet by providing them with a solution that is much better than taking additional supplements separately.

We do this by providing people with seasoning made from moringa which simply mixes in the flavour of the food you add it in. So, practically people will be eating the same food but their nutrition intake will be increased twice fold.

We also put immense importance on the cost of our products in order to make sure that they are within reach of common people. Currently, our products have 40% lower costs than our counterparts. Thus, we aim to provide people with a healthier, tastier and pocket-friendly alternative to Whey Proteins.

There are many interesting things that we plan to come up with. As of now, we are already started working on our next goal of using AI to produce 3 different kinds of seasoning products named(Sphura, Asavah and Tejomaya) which would further improve our competitive position.

IMPACT MISSION

Sustainable Livelihoods & Farmer's Empowerment

We are a mission-driven business who plan to use Moringa to empower farmers life and make them move up the value chain and thus giving them proper dignity, proper revenue and all the facilities they deserve for providing us with food.

We aim to create a just society where healthy food is available to everyone at a lower cost and subjects are nutritent deficiency are a matter of past. And thus every pack you buy will help a small scale farmer to grow. Thus, join us in the movement of making farming profitable again and nourishes people, both nutritionally and economically.
